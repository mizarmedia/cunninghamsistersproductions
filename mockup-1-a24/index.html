About the Film

"If we don't stand up for the children... then we don't stand up for much." - Marian W. Edelman

Resilience is a non-profit narrative feature film that exposes the systemic abuse within unregulated teen residential treatment programs. Inspired by real events, this gritty drama shines a light on an industry that has operated in the shadows for too long.

Through our 50/50 nonprofit revenue model, half of all proceeds fund initiatives to regulate troubled teen institutions, while the other half supports creating additional advocacy films.
